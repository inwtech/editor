Off the Wall ( 1979 )

Off the Wall was hailed as a major breakthrough for Jackson, while receiving critical recognition, along with praises, from major music publications.


Thriller ( 1982 )

Thriller was released on November 30, 1982, and sold one million copies worldwide per week at its peak. “Billie Jean” made Thriller a chart-topper.


Bad ( 1987 )

Bad made history as being the first album to have five of its singles peak at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 consecutively.


Dangerous ( 1991 )

After its first week of release, it debuted at number-one on the Billboard 200, staying there for four weeks, spanning two different calendar years.
